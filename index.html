Our Mission

To eliminate the disconnect between rural agricultural output and community nutritional needs by transforming clinical health data into actionable, precision-farming intelligence.

Our Vision

A resilient Northern Nigeria where every crop planted is strategically chosen to eradicate hidden hunger, optimize health outcomes, and empower local farmers economically.

The "Hidden Hunger" Crisis

Why producing more food isn't enough to solve malnutrition.

In regions like Azare, Bauchi State, we face a paradox: communities are surrounded by active agriculture, yet rural clinics consistently record high rates of micronutrient deficiencies, stunted growth, and anemia. This phenomenon, known as "hidden hunger," occurs when caloric intake is sufficient, but nutritional value is critically low.

Historically, agricultural planning has focused purely on yield and economic value, ignoring the specific health deficits of the population consuming the food. We realized that health data should not just be archived in hospital databases—it is the missing blueprint for agricultural strategy.

Our AgTech Architecture

How we create a continuous feedback loop from the clinic to the farm.

📱

1. Mobile Health Surveillance

We utilize Open Data Kit (ODK) and proprietary mobile tools at our digital health hubs to capture real-time, anonymized data on nutritional deficiencies across different rural wards.

💻

2. Data Processing & Analytics

Data is securely routed into a robust database architecture. Using Python-based analytics and customized algorithms, we map geographical hotspots of specific micronutrient needs.

🌱

3. Precision Crop Directives

This "Nutritional Intelligence" is translated into localized planting directives. We advise our farming partners on precisely which bio-fortified crops to cultivate to directly address the identified clinical deficits in their market.

Target Beneficiaries & Impact

A sustainable model designed for holistic community empowerment.

🌾 Smallholder Farmers

By providing data on exactly what crops are in high nutritional demand locally, we reduce post-harvest waste and guarantee a ready market for their produce, increasing their economic stability.

👩‍👧 Vulnerable Households

Directly combating maternal anemia and childhood stunting by ensuring the local food supply is rich in missing micronutrients like Iron and Vitamin A.

🚀 Scalability Roadmap

Beginning with a localized pilot in Azare alongside Fadaku Agro, our software infrastructure is designed to scale across Bauchi State and eventually integrate into wider Northern Nigerian agricultural policies.
